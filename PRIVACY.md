## FAB TCG Decklist Fabrary Exporter

This privacy policy describes how the FAB TCG Decklist Fabrary Exporter browser extension handles user information.

### Information Collection and Use

**The extension does NOT collect, store, or transmit any personal information.**

### What the Extension Does

The FAB TCG Decklist Fabrary Exporter:

1. **Reads publicly visible information** from fabtcg.com decklist pages that you visit
2. **Extracts card data** (card IDs, quantities, deck metadata) from the page content
3. **Generates a URL** for Fabrary's deck import feature
4. **Opens the URL** in a new browser tab

All processing happens locally in your browser. No data is sent to external servers, stored, or shared with third parties.

### Data Access

The extension only accesses:
- Content from pages matching `https://fabtcg.com/decklists/*`
- This access is limited to reading publicly visible decklist information

### No Tracking or Analytics

The extension does not:
- Track your browsing history
- Collect analytics or usage statistics
- Use cookies
- Store any user data
- Transmit data to external servers
- Use third-party analytics services

### Permissions Explained

**Host Permission for fabtcg.com:**
- **Why needed:** To read decklist information from fabtcg.com pages
- **What it accesses:** Only publicly visible card and deck information
- **What it does NOT do:** Does not access your account, login credentials, or any personal information

### Third-Party Services

When you click "Export to Fabrary," the extension opens Fabrary.net with a URL containing the decklist data. Once you are on Fabrary's website, Fabrary's own privacy policy applies. We have no control over and assume no responsibility for Fabrary's privacy practices.
